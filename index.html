How to Play

1

Play the Lessons

The tutorial starts inside the actual battlefield and gives you missions one at a time while you play.

2

Learn RTS Controls

You will select sheep, gather Marshmallows, build a Barracks, train units, and use attack-move.

3

Practice Building

The tutorial gives extra starting resources so you can build and train without waiting too long.

4

Finish the Mission

Train an army and destroy the enemy main base to complete the tutorial.

★

Pick Your Flock

Rainbow is expensive and magical. Mech is flexible and defensive. Fire expands quickly, and its buildings need Warm Wool.
